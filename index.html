About Me

Hello, my name is Anjan Bhattarai and I'm a creative designer currently based in Kathmandu. With expertise in Illustration, Web Design, Graphic Design, Advertising Design.
I've had the opportunity to work with some incredible companies and projects throughout my professional career. I believe that great design starts with a solid foundation, which is why I prioritize research and design thinking in my process. My design philosophy is centered around exploring new ideas and approaches, and I find inspiration in Music. I'm passionate about bringing ideas to life through design and creating meaningful experiences for my clients and their audiences. If you're interested in working with me, please don't hesitate to reach out via email or connect with me on my website or social media.
